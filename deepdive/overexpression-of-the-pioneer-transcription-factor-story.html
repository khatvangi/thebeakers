SCNT Challenges

Somatic cell nuclear transfer (SCNT) creates embryos by reprogramming donor cells, but epigenetic barriers often block normal development. These barriers make SCNT inefficient for cloning and regenerative medicine. Scientists are searching for ways to improve this process.

Nr5a2 as a Pioneer

The Nr5a2 protein acts like a 'key' to unlock gene expression by binding to tightly packed DNA. Overexpressing Nr5a2 helps reprogram SCNT embryos by targeting specific genes that are stuck in an inactive state.
