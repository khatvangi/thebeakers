Why Bromine Wins

Electrostatic Interactions

  • Larger ESP difference with PM6
  • Stronger donor-acceptor binding
  • Positive ESP at halogen sites

Film Morphology

  • Nanofibril structure (~27 nm)
  • Ideal phase separation
  • Higher crystallinity (CCL = 106 Å)

Charge Transport

  • μh = 6.51 × 10⁻⁴ cm²/Vs
  • μe = 4.10 × 10⁻⁴ cm²/Vs
  • Balanced μe/μh = 0.63

Carrier Dynamics

  • Lifetime: 6.52 μs (vs 1.3-1.8)
  • Fast extraction: 0.20 μs
  • Reduced recombination

Key Scientific Insight

Central-core halogenation follows the opposite rules compared to end-group halogenation. This opens entirely new molecular design strategies for high-performance NFAs.

End Groups (Traditional)
F > Cl >> Br
Central Core (New)
Br > F > Cl
