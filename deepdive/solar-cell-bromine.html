Key Terms

Non-Fullerene Acceptor (NFA)
Organic molecules that accept electrons in solar cells. They've replaced fullerene-based acceptors due to better light absorption and tunability.
Power Conversion Efficiency (PCE)
The percentage of sunlight energy converted to electricity. The holy grail metric for solar cells.
Electrostatic Potential (ESP)
A measure of how positive or negative different parts of a molecule are. Differences in ESP between donor and acceptor drive charge separation.
Carrier Lifetime
How long charge carriers (electrons/holes) survive before recombining. Longer = more charges collected = higher efficiency.
Nanofibril Morphology
A film structure with tiny fiber-like features that create efficient pathways for charge transport.
