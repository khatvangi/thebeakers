The Challenge

Current plastics cause environmental harm due to their non-biodegradable nature. Chitin derivatives offer a sustainable alternative by breaking down naturally without toxic residues.

The Method

Researchers synthesized chitin derivatives and tested their biodegradability and mechanical strength under various conditions. Advanced spectroscopy and microscopy techniques were used to analyze structural changes during degradation.

The Discovery

The derivatives degraded up to 85% faster than traditional plastics. They maintained structural integrity even after repeated use, showing potential for durable biodegradable products.
