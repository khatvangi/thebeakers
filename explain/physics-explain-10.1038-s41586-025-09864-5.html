The Hypothesis

Researchers proposed a new idea: using quantum geometry to create a chiral fermionic valve.

A chiral molecule is one that can't be superimposed on its mirror image, like a left and right hand. A fermionic particle is like an electron in physics.

By combining these two concepts, researchers hoped to create a device that could control the behavior of molecules with different properties.
