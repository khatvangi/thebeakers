The Challenge

Current LLMs struggle with persistent memory, accountability, and human-centric reasoning. They act as pattern-matchers, failing to adapt or understand context beyond surface-level interactions. These limitations hinder their ability to function as true AGI agents.

The Hypothesis

AGI requires an architectural 'gearbox' of metacognitive functions, not just scaled models. The proposed framework combines three components: a Persona Module for self-awareness, a Benchmarking Loop for self-correction, and FedRAG for persistent memory.
