The Challenge

Solar cell efficiency is crucial for renewable energy. Improving CIGS solar cells can lead to more cost-effective and sustainable power generation.

The Method

Researchers used SCAPS software to model CIGS solar cells with varying gallium content in the absorber layer. This allowed them to analyze the impact of gallium on device performance.

The Discovery

Increasing gallium content up to a certain point significantly enhanced the efficiency and power output of the simulated CIGS solar cells. The optimal gallium concentration for maximum performance was identified through simulation.
